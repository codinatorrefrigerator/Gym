Gym Workout

  1. 5-10 min warm up (cardio)

  2. Shoulder warm up

  3. pullups, 3 x 10-12

  4. Chest press machine, warm up x 2/ working sets 3 x 10-12 with heavier weight

  5. dumbell lateral raise 3 x 10-12

  6. Dumbell incline press 2 x warm up/ 3 x 10-12

  7. Wood cutter 4 x 25

  8. Leg press 2 x warm up/ 3 x 10-12

  9. Crunch machine 25 x

  10. Cardio cool down
